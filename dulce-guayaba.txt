dulce de guayaba is a sweet of guava that I love to do with my abuela

ingredients:

	guava (as much as you want)
	cinnanom
	panela

instructions

	put the guavas in boiling water until they are really soft.

	put the guava in the blender with little watter. We need that the mixture stays thick

	put the mixture in a recipient to boil with the cinnamon an panela and let it boil for many hours until it is like a paste
